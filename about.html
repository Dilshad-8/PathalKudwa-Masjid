About THEMosque

The land of Masjid, Pathal Kudwa was given by the Late Sufi Wali Muhammad in 1920. One half of his entire land was given as waqf to the mosque and later a small portion of the remaining land, (approximately 47 कड़ी), was given in the name of the late Ghulam Abbas, whom he established in the name of his family. Again he transferred the remaining portion of the land to his wife in the name of Late Saliman in the name of the mosque in the year 1949 (23.1.1949) so that till the time she was alive she would spend the rest of his life on house rent. After her death, the mosque committee will also include her land in the mosque. This land's खाता नंबर 2294 plot no. 1428 मौजा-कोनका , हलका-2. The total land area of the mosque is 4'5".

May Allah Grant Jannat-ul-Firdaus to Late Sufi Wali Muhammad and his wife Late Saliman. Ameen!

This Masjid was built in 1920 the foundation of masjid was made with Sand and Rubbish and periodically underwent some construction based on population in 1975. On 1975 second floor was constructed. The approximate area of the masjid is 3,270 Sqft.
