About THE Mosque

The land of Masjid, Pathal Kudwa was given by the Late Sufi Wali Muhammad in 1920. One half of his entire land was given as waqf to the mosque and later a small portion of the remaining land, (approximately 47 कड़ी), was given in the name of the late Ghulam Abbas, whom he established in the name of his family. Again he transferred the remaining portion of the land to his wife in the name of Late Saliman in the name of the mosque in the year 1949 (23.1.1949) so that till the time she was alive she would spend the rest of his life on house rent. After her death, the mosque committee will also include her land in the mosque. This land's खाता नंबर 2294 plot no. 1428 मौजा-कोनका , हलका-2. The total land area of the mosque is 4.5 कट्ठा".

May Allah Grant Jannat-ul-Firdaus to Late Sufi Wali Muhammad and his wife Late Saliman. Ameen!

The ongoing construction of the new Wazu Khana aims to provide a modern, spacious, and serene environment for the practice of ablution. Designed with both functionality and comfort.

The new Wazu Khana will not only enhance the ablution experience but also contribute to the overall spiritual atmosphere of the area, helping worshippers prepare in the most comfortable and respectful environment before prayer.
